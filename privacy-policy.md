Privacy Policy for BudgetCart
Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Information Collection: BudgetCart does not collect, store, or share any personal user data on external servers.
2. Camera Usage: The app requires Camera access solely to scan barcodes of grocery items. Images from the camera are processed locally on the device and are never uploaded or stored.
3. Data Storage: All shopping lists, prices, and budget settings are stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage.
4. Third-Party Services: We use the Open Food Facts API to retrieve product names. No personal identifiers are sent to this service.
5. Contact: For questions, contact kiransuresh495@gmail.com.
